ABOUT

Hi there, and welcome to my portfolio site! I'm Michelle, a wife, mom, lover of coffee, summer, and Cardinals baseball, and teacher-turned-web developer. I taught bilingual elementary students for 8 years, and then I decided to stay home and raise my 4 boys. During this time I tested websites and apps as a user experience tester, and I realized that I wanted to be the one behind the browser creating those engaging user experiences. I began teaching myself to code, and here I am more than a year later with Front-end development skills and several websites and apps to call my own. The next two items on my To Learn list are React and Ruby, so full-stack here I come!
